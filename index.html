About Us

Welcome to A K Barber Shop Northcliff — where style meets service.

We’re a friendly, professional barbershop serving Northcliff and the surrounding area. Whether you need a crisp men’s cut, neat school-ready cuts for the kids, or a fresh look for the ladies, our team is here to help.

From classic fades and blade work to eyebrow threading, hair colouring, and facials, we offer a full range of grooming and beauty services in one convenient spot. Drop in and see why our clients keep coming back.
